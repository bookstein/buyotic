Welcome to Buyotic


Without giving anything up, you and one million moms can protect your kids from drug-resistant superbugs.

The future of meat is at a tipping point... and we’re starting with chicken. Poultry farmers already know they don't need to feed antibiotics to healthy chickens -- endangering American families by creating drug-resistant superbugs. Who will prove to them that we don’t need to play chicken with our children's health?

We will.

Here's what YOU can do:

  1. Buy affordable, responsibly-raised certified chicken at a grocery store near you. We’ll help you find it.
  2. Did you find delicious chicken at a great price? Share with friends who care and reward the farmers that are doing it right!
  3. Your grocery store doesn’t carry antibiotic-free chicken? Send a message to the management and add your voice to a million-mom collective tweet.

Here’s what WE can do:

Buycott the good guys -- and make everyone want to get on board.

With our voices combined, we will convince grocery stores to stock healthy meats and reward responsible farmers. By acting together, we will make healthy, drug-free chicken available to everyone.
